Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How do I book a tour with Kenzy-tour?

▼

Booking with Kenzy-tour is simple! You can fill out our contact form with your travel preferences, and our team will get back to you with personalized options. Alternatively, you can call us directly at our toll-free number to speak with a travel consultant.

Can you customize tours based on my interests?

▼

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your specific interests, budget, and travel style. Whether you're interested in adventure activities, cultural experiences, culinary tours, or relaxation, we can design the perfect trip for you.

What is included in your tour packages?

▼

Our tour packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. The exact inclusions vary by package, and we provide detailed itineraries for each tour. We can also arrange additional services like travel insurance, visa assistance, and special activities upon request.

Do you offer travel insurance?

▼

While we don't directly provide travel insurance, we can recommend trusted insurance providers and help you select the right coverage for your trip. We strongly recommend all our clients purchase comprehensive travel insurance to protect against unexpected events.

What is your cancellation policy?

▼

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour and how far in advance you cancel. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days before departure receive a full refund minus a small administrative fee. Cancellations made closer to the departure date may incur higher fees. We provide detailed cancellation terms with each booking.
